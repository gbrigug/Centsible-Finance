About Us

Who are we?

As of now, there is only one contributor to this site. If you’d like to connect with me, my Twitter and LinkedIn are at the footer of every page. Eventually, I’d like to get other passionate individuals involved with this project.

What's on this site?

This site strives to provide free help to those wanting to achieve financial wellness. I want to provide a blanket of easy to understand information for specific terms, concepts, and strategies regarding personal finance.

Why does it exist?

This all started as a small personal web dev project. However, I felt that it had the potential to bring real value to people’s lives, so I decided to stick with it. I’m a strong believer that financial wellness is just a series of small steps. I want to help people take those steps. I also felt that there just wasn’t an influx of good resources available.

Is this really free?

Yes. I found that a lot of the information available on the Internet often deals with get rich quick schemes, individuals selling their own products or services, or other other shady deals. While there are some legitimate paid resources, I wanted to try and create a centralized place for some basic information.
